2.4.1. TAB “Procedure”¶

The measurement problem to be considered can be characterized here by a short description of the method`s principle and special characteristics may be pointed at. Cited literature may also be included. If the project is under development regarding the equations, this TAB may be skipped.

When the text input is finished one may change by mouse click to the TAB “Equations” .
